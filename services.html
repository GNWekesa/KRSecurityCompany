Our Services

Manned Guarding Services

We provide highly trained and licensed security personnel to protect your premises, assets, and people. Our guards are skilled in access control, incident response, and maintaining a visible security presence.

24/7 Alarm Response and Monitoring

Our rapid-response team is on standby 24/7 to respond to alarm activations. Coupled with advanced monitoring systems, we ensure immediate action to potential threats or breaches.

CCTV Surveillance Systems

We offer cutting-edge CCTV installation and monitoring solutions. Our surveillance systems are designed to deter criminal activity and provide real-time or recorded video evidence when needed.

Event Security Management

From corporate events to large-scale public gatherings, we deliver tailored security strategies. Our event security personnel ensure crowd control, emergency preparedness, and smooth event operations.

VIP Protection & Escort Services

Discreet and reliable personal protection for executives, dignitaries, and celebrities. Our close protection officers are trained to handle high-risk situations while maintaining client privacy and safety.

Security Risk Assessment & Consultancy

We analyze vulnerabilities in your physical and operational environment and provide expert recommendations to enhance security. Our consultants tailor risk mitigation plans specific to your needs.

Access Control and Biometric Systems

Implement state-of-the-art access control systems, including biometric technology, to secure entry points and manage personnel flow. These systems increase accountability and restrict unauthorized access.
